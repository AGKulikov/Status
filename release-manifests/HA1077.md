baseline. It does not contain the experimental embedded navigator work.

## Included fixes

- Retain the active MediaSession token when ECARX/Yandex returns the same
  playing sessions in a different order.
- Prevent a newer rich-media track from being rolled back by a late stale
  MediaSession poll, while releasing a silent broadcaster after an 8-second
  conflict grace period.
- Keep title, artist, album, artwork, play/pause and timeline on one coherent
  track when MediaSession and the mHUD-compatible broadcast disagree.
- Skip no-op artwork and play/pause rendering on one-second position ticks,
  without retaining controller-owned bitmaps after they may be recycled.
- Restart marquee only for the individual title, artist or album line whose
  text or marquee mode actually changed.
- Resize every launcher panel from any of its four visible corners while the
  diagonally opposite corner stays fixed; preserve grid snapping, minimum
  dimensions, workspace bounds and persisted geometry.
